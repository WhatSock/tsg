Tooltip Internal Content

The tooltip is optimized for screen readers and keyboard only users:

  • Set focus on one of the Focus fields, then press Tab and Shift+Tab to navigate between them.
  • Tabbing away from an active element with a tooltip, or pressing Escape, will close the tooltip.
  • Mouse users can mouse in and out of the icon to show or hide the tooltip as usual.

Important:

  • Must not contain special characters.
